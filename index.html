A note from our founder
DL
Daniel Lynton
Founder and CEO, Lynton

When I started Lynton in 1999, software was something you crafted. The technology a company ran was a direct reflection of its ambition. For our first decade, we helped companies build systems they truly owned.

Then SaaS arrived. We shifted with it. We became one of the most technical HubSpot partners on the planet, building custom integrations, pioneering apps, pushing the platform further than almost anyone. For sixteen years, we believed that was the path forward.

"The architecture of monolithic all-in-one platforms carries inherent ceilings by design. We watched company after company hit them."

Those years taught us what bad looks like. Where systems break. And what to replace them with. AI didn't just change what we can build, it changed the math entirely. Custom, open-source infrastructure is now accessible at any scale. The world's biggest brands already run on it. Now any business can.

We've rebuilt Lynton around a simple belief: the path forward leads back to something familiar. Software shaped around the way your business actually works, owned by the people who depend on it. The difference now is that AI rewrote the rules for what a small team can build.

Let's build software we own again.

Daniel Lynton, Founder and CEO
